What is PozzoDyne™?

Ultra-fine. Engineered. Repeatable.

PozzoDyne™ is an ultra-fine ground glass pozzolan manufactured from carefully processed glass feedstocks. Through AggrePlex's proprietary material processing, the glass is cleaned, micronized, and classified into a precise particle-size distribution that delivers high performance in cementitious systems.

As a supplementary cementitious material, PozzoDyne™ can replace 20–30% of ordinary Portland cement in the concrete matrix — reducing embodied carbon while maintaining or improving concrete performance.

PozzoDyne™ is designed to support:
Lower-carbon concrete
Enhanced long-term strength development
Improved durability
Increased sulfate resistivity
Reduced chloride permeability
Circular use of glass byproducts
ASR mitigation

Engineered reactivity. Measurable results. How PozzoDyne™ concrete outperforms conventional mixes.

01

20–30% cement replacement

Comparable or improved compressive strength with standard mix design practices — no production redesign.

02

Lower embodied carbon

Direct CO₂ reduction per cubic yard; supports ESG, regulatory, and project-specific carbon targets.

03

Reduced permeability

Ultra-fine particles refine the pore structure — lower chloride ingress and improved durability in aggressive environments.

04

ASR & sulfate resistance

Consumes free lime into additional C-S-H, limiting expansive alkali-silica and sulfate reactions.

05

Strength at every age

Slag-like early performance, ~100% of control at 28 days, and continued densification beyond 56 days.

06

Thermal performance

A denser matrix limits heat transfer — higher R-value, lower U-value, better energy efficiency.

07

Fire & heat stability

Less free lime and more stable C-S-H phases reduce high-temperature degradation and spalling risk.

08

Acoustic & consistency

Higher sound attenuation (STC), plus engineered repeatability that eliminates the variability of traditional SCMs.

CO₂ reduction through cement replacement

Cement is the most carbon-intensive component of concrete. Replacing 20–30% of it with PozzoDyne™ directly lowers embodied carbon per cubic yard — without changing how concrete is produced.

Data-backed decarbonization

LCA-validated performance supports ESG and sustainability reporting, aligns with emerging low-carbon material standards, and gives project owners quantifiable impact.

Circular material strategy

Landfill-bound glass becomes a high-value cementitious input — upgrading existing material streams, extending lifecycles, and reducing dependency on newly produced materials.

Certifications & Standards

Built to be specified.

PozzoDyne™ is engineered to meet established industry standards and integrate seamlessly into existing concrete specifications.

ASTM C1866 Compliance

Standard Specification for Ground-Glass Pozzolan for Use in Concrete

Loss on Ignition (LOI) ≤ 0.5% — consistently below threshold
Fineness — engineered particle-size distributions well within specification
Chemical composition — high amorphous silica content suitable for pozzolanic reaction
Performance criteria — meets or exceeds required strength activity and durability benchmarks

Specification-ready for commercial and infrastructure projects — can be specified as a ground-glass pozzolan per ASTM C1866 within performance-based specs and 20–30% replacement strategies.

Performance-Based Validation

Evaluated through a comprehensive testing framework aligned with industry standards

Reactivity & strength — ASTM C311 (Strength Activity Index), C1897 (Isothermal Calorimetry)
Durability — ASTM C1260 / C1567 (ASR mitigation), C1012 (sulfate resistance), C1202 (chloride penetration)
